# 🌌 ABILITY: Gravity & Time

**Immersive Focus with the Force of Gravity**

ABILITY: Gravity & Time is not just a utility; it is a "Superpower" in your pocket. It is the first installment of the **'ABILITY'** series—a minimal productivity toolset designed to give users extraordinary control over their daily routines.

This project redefines the **"immersive experience"** of using a timer. While conventional timers are mere tools that display decreasing numbers, ABILITY provides an experience where the user becomes a "master of time," deploying and sustaining a gravitational field to protect their focus.

The core values of the product are:

- Providing a visual flow of gravity instead of dry, static numbers.
- Ensuring "Continuous Immersion" by preventing the screen from dimming during focus.
- Transforming "evaporating efforts" into valuable personal assets through statistics.

---

## Project Background — Focus as an "Ability"

This project originated from questions regarding the "boredom" and "unfriendliness" of existing productivity tools.

### The "Boring" UX
Most timers are visually static, offering no visual feedback or motivation during the concentration period. Focus should not be painful endurance; it should be an engaging state of flow.

### The "Black Screen" Anxiety
Standard timers allow the screen to turn off mid-session. The moment a user touches their phone to check their progress, their flow is broken. I wanted to eliminate this friction.

### Ghost Efforts
The moment a timer ends, the effort of that session often vanishes into thin air. I recognized the need for data that allows users to visually track how well they have controlled their "Gravity (Focus)" daily and weekly.

To address these pain points, I arrived at the conclusion: **"Translate focus into the superpower of Gravity."**

---

## Problems Addressed

### Visualizing Focus: The Gravity Field UI
→ **Temporal Core + Physics-based UI**

Instead of viewing time as mere digits, I implemented a visual experience where users drag a "Core" to set the temporal mass. The premium Deep Grey aesthetic minimizes eye strain while maximizing immersion.

---

### Preventing Interruption: Persistent Display System
→ **Wakelock Integration**

I implemented a feature that prevents the screen from sleeping during focus sessions. Users can monitor their "Gravity Status" (remaining time) with a quick glance without touching the device, keeping their flow intact.

---

### The Guaranteed Period: Native Alert System
→ **Android Native Alarm Manager + Haptics**

To solve the issue of notifications being suppressed by background battery optimization, I utilized the Native Alarm Manager. The moment the gravity field stabilizes (timer ends), it delivers unmistakable feedback through powerful haptics and deep ambient sound.

---

### Capitalizing Effort: Focus Statistics
→ **Daily/Weekly Stats Visualization**

Every completed session is recorded in a local database. By viewing their "Time Mastery" stats, users gain a sense of achievement and the energy required for their next session.

---

## Technical Decision Rationale

- **Flutter & Custom Painting** Chosen to break away from standardized UI and implement unique graphics and animations that align with the "Superpower" concept.

- **Wakelock API** Technically resolved the UX issue of screen timeouts, ensuring the user’s immersion remains unbroken throughout the session.

- **Android Native Alarm Manager** Applied `exactAllowWhileIdle` and `alarmClock` modes to ensure 100% notification reliability even when the app is in the background or in power-saving mode.

- **AdMob (Rewarded Ads) - Energy System** Instead of intrusive banner ads, I implemented a "Rewarded Ad" model framed as an "Energy Recharge." This ensures monetization is woven into the app's lore without harming the UX.
